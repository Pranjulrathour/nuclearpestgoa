## Understanding Termite Problems in Goa

### Why Goa Has Severe Termite Issues:

1. **High Humidity** - Perfect breeding conditions
2. **Coastal Climate** - Moisture-rich environment
3. **Wooden Structures** - Abundant food source
4. **Monsoon Rains** - Increased termite activity
5. **Old Portuguese Architecture** - Wooden elements vulnerable

### Types of Termites in Goa:

#### 1. **Subterranean Termites** (Most Common)
- Live underground in soil
- Build mud tubes
- Most destructive species
- Attack wooden structures from below

#### 2. **Drywood Termites**
- Live inside wood
- No soil contact needed
- Attack furniture and doors
- Leave powdery droppings

#### 3. **Dampwood Termites**
- Prefer wet, rotting wood
- Common in monsoon season
- Attack moist structures
- Less common than subterranean

## Termite Prevention Tips for Goa Homes

### 1. **Moisture Control**
- Fix leaky pipes immediately
- Ensure proper drainage
- Use dehumidifiers in damp areas
- Ventilate bathrooms and kitchens
- Fix roof leaks promptly

### 2. **Wood Protection**
- Keep wood away from soil contact
- Store firewood away from house
- Use termite-resistant wood
- Apply wood preservatives
- Regular wood inspection

### 3. **Home Maintenance**
- Seal cracks in foundation
- Remove dead trees/stumps
- Clear debris around property
- Maintain 6-inch clearance (wood to soil)
- Regular pest inspections

### 4. **Landscaping**
- Keep plants away from walls
- Don't use wood mulch near foundation
- Ensure soil slopes away from house
- Trim tree branches touching roof
- Remove wooden landscape features

### 5. **Storage Practices**
- Don't store cardboard on floor
- Keep stored items away from walls
- Use plastic containers, not cardboard
- Elevate storage shelves
- Regular cleaning and inspection
