## Understanding the Rodent Problem in Goa

### Why Rodents Are Common in Goa:

1. **Coastal Environment** - Abundant food and water sources
2. **Dense Population** - Urban areas provide shelter
3. **Food Industry** - Restaurants, hotels, warehouses
4. **Monsoon Season** - Rodents seek dry shelter
5. **Old Buildings** - Portuguese architecture with entry points
6. **Markets and Waste** - Easy food access

### Common Rodent Species in Goa:

#### 1. **Roof Rats (Black Rats)**
- Most common in Goa
- Excellent climbers
- Live in upper areas
- Black/dark brown color
- Long tail, pointed nose

#### 2. **Norway Rats (Brown Rats)**
- Larger than roof rats
- Burrow in ground
- Prefer lower areas
- Brown/gray color
- Heavy, thick body

#### 3. **House Mice**
- Smallest rodent pest
- Very fast breeders
- Gray/light brown
- Small, pointed features
- Invade homes easily
