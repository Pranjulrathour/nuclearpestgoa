## Bed Bug Prevention Tips for Goa Properties

### For Homeowners:

#### **Travel Precautions:**
- Inspect hotel rooms before unpacking
- Keep luggage on stands, not floor
- Check bed seams and headboards
- Seal luggage in plastic during travel
- Wash all clothes in hot water after travel
- Inspect luggage before bringing inside

- Use mattress and box spring encasements
- Reduce clutter (fewer hiding spots)

## **Bed Bug Infestation** Myths vs Facts

### ‚ùå **Myth 1:** Bed bugs only infest dirty places
‚úÖ **Fact:** Bed bugs are found in 5-star hotels too. They don't care about cleanliness.

### ‚ùå **Myth 2:** You can see all bed bugs easily
‚úÖ **Fact:** Nymphs (babies) are almost invisible. Eggs are microscopic.

### ‚ùå **Myth 3:** Bed bugs only live in beds
‚úÖ **Fact:** They hide in furniture, walls, electrical outlets, anywhere within 20 feet of sleeping areas.

### ‚ùå **Myth 4:** Bed bugs fly or jump
‚úÖ **Fact:** Bed bugs crawl. They can't fly or jump but can move quickly.

‚úÖ **Fact:** Professional treatments have 90-100% success rates vs. 20-40% for DIY.
